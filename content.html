My Website

In a room where the screen softly glows,
I type away as the evening flows.
With every line, a world I create,
In logic and loops, I find my fate.

The world may rush, the hours may fly,
But code is where my soul can lie.
Like magic built from thoughts and keys,
I solve, I build, I debug with ease.

My heart beats fast for a land afar,
Where minarets shine like a silver star.
Turkey—land of tulips and tales,
Where beauty breathes through ancient trails.

Yet here I stand on familiar ground,
In Pakistan where roots are found.
A place of strength, of song, of grace,
Where dreams still bloom in a quiet place.

I carry both in who I am—
A coder, a dreamer, a bridge, a man.
With Türkiye in my dreams at night,
And Pakistan beneath daylight.

So here's to lines of endless code,
And roads that lead where hopes explode.
A life between two worlds I see—
One of passion, pride, and possibility.
